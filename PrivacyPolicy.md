# Privacy Policy for FrameGlimpse



## 1. Our Privacy Principles

FrameGlimpse is an on-device media utility that helps you find and save moments from videos and Live Photos, and review old screenshots or similar photos.

We follow these principles:

- On-device processing: media analysis and cleanup happen on your device.
- No media uploads: we do not upload your photos, videos, Live Photos, or screenshots to our servers.
- No account required: you do not need to register or sign in.
- No automatic deletion: anything deleted must be selected and confirmed by you.
- No automatic full-library iCloud downloads: only individual items you explicitly choose may be downloaded when needed.

---

## 2. Content We May Access

After you grant permission, the App may access photos, videos, Live Photos, and screenshots within the scope you allow.

This access is used to:

- capture photos from videos frame by frame;
- select better moments from Live Photos;
- recommend moments from videos on device;
- identify old screenshots;
- identify similar photos;
- display and manage the media currently accessible to the App.

If you use iOS Limited Photos access, the App can only access the items you choose to allow.

---

## 3. Media Metadata

To support frame capture, recommendations, cleanup, and saving, the App may read necessary media metadata on device, such as:

- capture date and time;
- location information;
- file type;
- dimensions and resolution;
- video duration;
- Live Photo information.

This information is used only for local App functionality and is not uploaded to our servers.

---

## 4. iCloud Media

If a photo, video, or Live Photo is stored only in iCloud, the App will not automatically download your full iCloud library or automatically batch-download all iCloud media.

Only when you explicitly choose to view, analyze, or process a specific item may the App request the system to download that individual item to your device.

If an operation may use cellular data, the App will try to show a notice before continuing. You may cancel or stop the operation.

---

## 5. Saving and Deleting

### 5.1 Saving

When you choose to save a photo or Live Photo, the App asks the system to write the item you confirmed into your Photos library.

### 5.2 Deleting

When you use cleanup features to delete old screenshots or similar photos:

- the App does not delete anything automatically;
- you must actively select items;
- deletion requires confirmation;
- deleted items are typically moved to the “Recently Deleted” album in the Photos app, subject to iOS system behavior.

---

## 6. Data We Do Not Collect

Unless this policy is updated in the future, the current version of FrameGlimpse:

- does not collect your name, email, phone number, or account information;
- does not collect advertising identifiers;
- does not use third-party advertising SDKs;
- does not use third-party analytics SDKs;
- does not upload photos, videos, or Live Photos;
- does not track you across apps or websites;
- does not sell personal data.

---

## 7. Third-Party Services

The current version of the App does not rely on third-party servers to process your media content.

Please note that app download, installation, crash diagnostics, payments, refunds, ratings, and reviews may be handled by Apple’s App Store, iOS, or related Apple services. Such processing is governed by Apple’s terms and privacy policy and is outside our direct control.

---

## 8. Children’s Privacy

FrameGlimpse provides local media processing features for general users. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children, and the App does not require users to provide an account, name, email address, or other identity information.

If you believe we have inadvertently collected personal information from a child, please contact us using the details below.

---

## 9. Data Security

We reduce privacy risks by:

- processing media on device where possible;
- not creating an account system;
- not uploading your photos or videos;
- not automatically downloading your full iCloud library;
- not automatically deleting any content;
- requiring user confirmation for key actions such as downloading, saving, and deleting.

However, no system can be guaranteed to be completely secure. You should also protect your device, iCloud account, and system permission settings.
